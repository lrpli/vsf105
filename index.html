Flying Above the Volunteer State

Volunteer State Flight 105 is a community of aviators, student pilots, and aviation enthusiasts rooted in Lenoir City, Tennessee. We believe the sky is for everyone — and we work every day to prove it.

From introductory Young Eagles flights that give kids their first experience in the air, to advanced training support and fly-ins that bring the East Tennessee aviation community together, VSF105 is where passion meets purpose.

Our Programs

From first flights to advanced training, VSF105 supports aviators at every stage of their journey.

Flight Training

We connect aspiring pilots with experienced CFIs, provide ground school resources, and support members through every step of earning their certificates.

See All Programs →

Young Eagles

Volunteer pilots give local youth ages 8–17 their very first airplane ride — completely free. Every flight plants a seed for the next generation of aviators.

Fly-Ins & Events

We host and participate in regional fly-ins, pancake breakfasts, airshows, and community events that celebrate aviation culture across East Tennessee.
